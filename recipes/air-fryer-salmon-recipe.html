Air Fryer Salmon Recipe

Description

Today we go back to the air fryer and show you our salmon air fryer recipe! This recipe works with any fish, not only salmon. But salmon especially turns out great this way. We use a Ninja Foodi Two Basket air fryer but all you need is an air fryer, some fish, and a few ingredients. Enjoy :)

Ingredients

  • 2 filet of salmon (around 6-8 oz each)
  • 1 teaspoon coconut oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon lemon pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dill
  • 1 grated lemon rind
