Ted Lasso Biscuit Recipe

Description

One of our favorite TV shows is Ted Lasso on Apple TV+. We’re watching season 2 week by week and had an epiphany: we should make the Ted Lasso biscuit recipe for our channel! Join Magda and Nathaniel as they show you how to make this quick, yummy, and buttery biscuit Ted Lasso style! Go watch Ted Lasso and munch on these shortcakes because you’ll love them and you’ll feel like Ted Lasso himself (or Rebecca).

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup almond flour
  • 2 sticks unsalted butter (chilled and the cut into small cubes)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extact
