Air Fried General Tso’s Chicken Recipe

Description

Today we are showing you how to make a classic General Tso's Chicken in the Air Fryer (our favorite kitchen device)! This recipe tastes exactly like something you get from your local Chinese restaurant. We make this with our Ninja Foodi Two Basket Air Fryer but you can make it with any air fryer. Enjoy the flavors of this tasty dish and the simplicity of the air fryer.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound boneless, skingless, chicken thighs (cut into 1 to 1 1/4 inch piece
  • 1 large egg (beaten)
  • 1/2 cup cornstarch
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon hot read pepper flakes
  • 1 tablespoon minced, peeled fresh ginger
  • 1 cup chicken broth (low sodium)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce (low sodium)
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons unseasoned rice vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons scallions or green onions (sliced)
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 to 1 teaspoon sesame seeds (roasted)

Directions

Visual learner? No problem! Watch the full recipe here.

  1. Pat chicken dry. Combine chicken and egg in a large bowl; toss to coat. In another bowl, combine ½ cornstarch, salt, and pepper. Using a fork, transfer chicken to cornstarch mixture; stir to coat.
  2. Preheat air fryer to 290°F. Transfer chicken to air fryer, leaving space between pieces. Cook for 15 minutes, shaking every 5 minutes. Let dry 3 to 5 minutes. If chicken is still damp, cook 1 to 2 more minutes.
  3. Heat canola oil and red pepper flakes in a skillet over medium. Add ginger and cook until fragrant, around 30 seconds.
  4. Whisk together broth, soy sauce, ketchup, sugar, rice vinegar, and remaining 2 teaspoons cornstarch; stir into skillet. Increase heat to medium high, and cook until mixture begins to bubble. Add chicken, and stir to coat. Cook until thickened, about one minute and 30 seconds. Turn off heat; stir in 1 tablespoon scallions and sesame oil. Top with sesame seeds and remaining 1 tablespoon scallions.
